## What It Does

CyberGuard IR Assistant guides you through every phase of a security incident using Claude AI as your expert advisor. Whether you're dealing with ransomware, a data breach, or unauthorized access — it gives you structured, technical guidance in real time.

**IR Phases covered:**
- 🔍 Detection & Analysis
- 🛡️ Containment
- 🧹 Eradication
- 🔄 Recovery
- 📋 Post-Incident Review

## Getting Started

### 1. Open the app
Visit: **https://triniviking.github.io/cyberguard-ir-assistant**

### 2. Get a free API key
- Go to [console.anthropic.com](https://console.anthropic.com)
- Sign up for a free account
- Click **API Keys → Create Key**
- Copy the key (starts with `sk-ant-`)

### 3. Enter your key
Click **API KEY** in the top-right corner of the app and paste your key. It's saved in your browser only — never sent to any server.

### 4. Open an incident
Fill in the form on the left:
- Give the incident a title
- Select the incident type (Ransomware, Phishing, Data Breach, etc.)
- Set the severity (Critical / High / Medium / Low)
- Describe the scope and what was observed
- Click **OPEN INCIDENT**

The AI will immediately provide expert guidance for the Detection phase.
